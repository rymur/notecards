Welcome

This site lets you make and share flashcards with other users. Features include:

  • Create your own flashcard decks, up to 100 decks!
  • Browse other users' decks and copy the ones you like
  • Don't want other users to see your deck? No problem, the site allows you to "unpublish" decks so that other users can't see them
  • Add, edit, and delete cards
  • Study your flashcards using an algorithm that selects your hardest cards more often
  • There's also a "hard mode" that only drills you on your weakest cards

Join today! You can even use your pre-existing Google account to log in!
